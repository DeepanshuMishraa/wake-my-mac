## Design Context

### Users
Mac users who want a calmer, more delightful way to manage attention and interruptions while working. The website is a public marketing and conversion surface for the Hold My Lid macOS utility.

### Brand Personality
Playful, polished, calm. The interface should feel native to macOS, quietly clever, and trustworthy rather than loud or overly technical.

### Aesthetic Direction
Refined minimalism inspired by Alcove's sparse, product-first landing page: generous white space, expressive display typography, a warm lime-green accent, dark product-demo moments, soft organic forms, and a clear download/purchase path. Prefer light mode with dark demo sections. Avoid generic SaaS dashboards, neon gradients, noisy backgrounds, and dense copy.

### Design Principles
- Make the product visual and immediately understandable.
- Use restraint: one dominant accent, few words, and generous breathing room.
- Let playful interaction and motion reward curiosity without harming accessibility.
- Keep conversion paths obvious and repeated only when useful.
- Treat SEO as part of the design: semantic structure, descriptive copy, metadata, and fast-loading assets.
